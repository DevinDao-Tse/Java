### Exponential Functions

Let $f(x) = a^x$, what is $f^\prime(x)$

$$
f^\prime(x)=\lim_{h\to 0}\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}

=\frac{a^{x+h} - a^x}{h}
=\frac{a^xa^h-a^x}{h}
$$
